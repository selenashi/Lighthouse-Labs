Mexican Rice and Bean Burritos


Description

Delicious and easy spicy bean and rice burritos

Ingredients

  • 1 can Black Beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup Brown Rice, cooked
  • 1/4 cup Fresh Salsa
  • 3 Whole Tortillas, warmed
  • 1 cup Shredded Lettuce
  • 1 tablespoon Packaged Burrito Sauce

Directions

  1. In a sauce pan, add in drianed and rinsed beans and water
  2. Cook briefly about 1 minute
  3. Add the packaged burrito sauce and cooked rice
  4. Bring to a simmer, then lower heat to medium and cook for 5 minutes
  5. Remove from heat, stir in salsa
  6. Divide mixture evenly at the center of the tortilla
  7. Top with lettuce and salsa
